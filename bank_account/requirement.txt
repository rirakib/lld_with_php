1. Class Design
Base Class: Account

Properties: accountNumber, balance

Methods: deposit($amount), withdraw($amount), getBalance()

Child Classes:

SavingsAccount

Special Rule: একবারে withdraw করতে পারবে max 20,000 টাকা।

CurrentAccount

Special Rule: overdraft allow করবে (balance negative হতে পারবে, যেমন –5000 পর্যন্ত)।


Expected Output (Example Scenario)

Savings Account Balance: 15000
Withdraw 18000 → Success
Withdraw 25000 → Error: Exceeds withdraw limit

Current Account Balance: 5000
Withdraw 8000 → Success (Balance = -3000)
Withdraw 7000 → Error: Overdraft limit exceeded
